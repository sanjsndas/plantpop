Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Like the delicate patterns on butterfly wings, our use of cookies creates a seamless interaction while respecting your privacy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information such as login status, preferences, and analytic data.

Our Use of Cookies Includes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site security and function.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data to optimize site speed and usage.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences like language and regional settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by third parties to display relevant ads respecting your choices.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or via cookie banners where applicable.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Data sharing is limited to trusted entities to support our service or comply with legal obligations.

Who We Share Data With:

  • Service providers assisting with payments, analytics, hosting, and communications.
  • Compliance authorities if legally compelled or for protection against fraud/abuse.
  • Business partners when authorized by you or under contractual obligation to protect your data.

All third parties are contractually required to keep your information confidential and use it solely for the agreed purposes.

Data Security Measures

As guardians of your data, we employ multi-layered security inspired by nature’s protective mechanisms.

  • Encrypted transmission using TLS for all data between your device and our servers.
  • Secure server environments with strict access controls and regular vulnerability testing.
  • Authentication protocols and periodic audits to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Employee training and data handling policies ensuring confidentiality.

Despite best efforts, no system is perfectly secure; we advise responsible data sharing and immediate notification of any suspicious activity.
